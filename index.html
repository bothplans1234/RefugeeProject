Is there syrian refugees in Tunisia?

  • Yes there is


    According to Realites online, Lorena Lando, president of the International Organization for Migration in Tunisia, told the Assembly of People's Representatives that the number of refugees currently Tunisia, is nearly 775, mostly Syrians, or 0.5% of the indigenous population.

  • Who are we?


    Refugee is an association which members who carry out actions and cooperates with several organizations to help Syrian refugees. Volunteers are often used to organize events to help them become part of society.
